FACULTY ADVISORS

Ryan young leaders model United nations is not just an MUN, it's an entire community, it is a movement, being a part of which will help you grow into a responsible, diplomatic, rational and practical person. In today's global world, RYLMUN has been designed to provide students with the best possible platform to voice their opinions about important issues. Whether it be the executive board members, the organizing committee members or the in charges, our goal has always been to prioritize you and your needs and work diligently to give you the best possible experience. Needless to say, RYLMUN is a place where you will be seen, heard and understood. Partaking in this event will not only broaden your wisdom by introducing you to unique concepts and getting you to debate on them, it will also force you to push your limits and uplift spontaneity and inculcate in you the art of quick thinking. Having been a part of ryan young leaders model United nation for quite some time now, I can proudly say that RYLMUN is indeed the best of the best. We always have, and still continue to work towards our one aim, giving you an experience that you will remember and cherish forever.

Nisha Sharma

Principal

I have been with RYLMUN since the very beginning. I cannot describe in words how much I have learnt and how much more i am yet to explore. Watching the students giving nothing but their best, debating restlessly in their respective committees to get a fruitful outcome, seeing the executive board members working hard to ensure the smooth functioning of the committees and above all, watching the backbone, the organizers of the MUN work with determination to provide us with the best possible conference throughout all the editions has been a delight. The members, the delegates and the in charges may have changed over the years, but let me tell you, the quality and finesse of RYLMUN never will. It has been and always will be a scintillating experience for all.

Ms. Sabahat Zaidi

Project In-Charge
