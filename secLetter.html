LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

Dear Participants,

On behalf of my entire Secretariat, it is my absolute honor to invite you all to the 5th Edition of the Ryan Young Leaders’ Model United Nations Conference, organized by Ryan International School, Faridabad. Since the first-ever edition of our conference, the Secretariat has worked harder each year to bring worldwide issues to a platform on which they can be discussed freely to raise awareness through the community and to provide the highest-quality conference, no matter the circumstances. Our goal has always been to challenge and support young leaders by providing them with an opportunity to discuss real issues with an emphasis on fostering the values of effective leadership in our ever-changing world.
A very essential learning that I have had through years of being a part of this activity is that Model United Nations experiences make participants aware of how their actions, words, or objectives, even those they perceive as rather trivial, can become a relevant piece of the ever-changing puzzle of our world. RYLMUN is not only a conference that will benefit you on an academic level, considering your opportunity to expand your knowledge and utilize it in engaging debates but it is also your chance to meet new people and create unforgettable memories. Prepare and embolden yourself to engage in world-changing debates as you seize this amazing opportunity.
RYLMUN has always been a source of constant learning and has fascinated me deeply, ever since I first heard about it. Therefore, being able not only to participate in it but also to organize it with my colleagues is an honor I will never be too grateful for. Leading such a prestigious and impactful conference is sure to be an immense undertaking, and I hope to do so with as much enthusiasm, wisdom, and support as I can while following in the great legacies left behind by the previous editions.
In keeping with our objectives, we look forward to opening our doors to the hundreds of students who each year reinforce a diversity of thought through constructive, thought-provoking discussions. RYLMUN, following its objectives, will continue to mentor delegates in leadership, diplomacy, and cooperation and serve as an opportunity for all to engage with our consequential educational mission, and will serve as a trendsetter for best practices in Model UN conferences across the country.

I look forward to your active and vital participation on the 22nd & 23rd of July 2022!

Yours Faithfully,
Vedansh Arora
Secretary-General
Ryan Young Leaders’ Model United Nations Conference 2022
