SECRETARIAT

It has been an honor to be associated with RYLMUN, a conference that has always been synonymous with perfection, created a platform for mentoring delegates in leadership, diplomacy, and cooperation, and has constantly been a trendsetter for others to follow. It has aimed to support young leaders in developing themselves and is a perfect balance of debate, diplomacy, and not to forget, lots of fun.

Vedansh Arora
Secretary General

RYLMUN has been an integral part of my school life from delegating to organizing the MUN. I have become a responsible and confident individual. Being a part of Ryan Faridabad MUN society for 5 years now, I understand how much effort RYLMUN put forward for every participant to give them the best experience.

Abhinav Dutta
Director General

I feel privileged and honoured to be the deputy director general of RYLMUN 2022. Doing MUN has taught me what no textbook could teach: on a smaller scale, a MUN to me is a way to bridge gaps. I believe MUNs are the means through which we, the future, carve out our ideas in the present. Voicing opinion is an integral part of humanity – this is what provides us with individuality. Awareness and understanding are what we seek, and MUN is an activity that provides precisely these things I, as the deputy director general, expect an exemplary debate and a lot of enthusiasm from the delegates, and I in turn guarantee you loads of memories to cherish. I promise to contribute to the conference to the best of my ability and make it a memorable two-day journey for you all.

Riya
Deputy Director General

I have participated in RYLMUN for as long as I was eligible, and this is my first time on the organizing committee. I have seen the RYLMUN conference grow and reach so many young minds and I hope with this edition we can help out and make this experience a far more memorable one for all the participants. And I can say that RYLMUN has been a life-changing experience for many, including myself, so it is my advice to all the juniors to take part.

Arushi Khare
Charge D’Affaires

The beginning is the most important part of the work. I understand how important it is to hold our spirits high. We are strongly committed to providing complete support to all in this edition of RYLMUN’22, guided by our shared values. The world needs your energy, and I draw great hope from seeing you mobilize to address the challenges of our time and build a healthier, more equitable, and sustainable future for all. RYMUN has been an integral part of my school life. Seeking further to see more creative minds and work with great students and teachers.

Tulip Chaterjee
Charge D’Affaires

RYLMUN has been an important part of my MUN journey. It holds a special place in my heart, being my very first MUN and after being part of it for some time now and being part of the Organising Committee is a privilege, and learning to be open and confident has been my best lesson.

Sambhavi Sharma
USG IP

Being a part of Ryan Faridabad and having participated in numerous RYLMUN Conferences has been a great experience full of learnings for me. We shall therefore once again put in all the efforts to make it a memorable experience for every participant as it's what RYLMUN has been known for.

Ranjul Chaudhary
USG Logistics

MUN has played a big role in my life. It helped me build up my personality and, everyone does deserve such a great experience hence, we would be doing our best to make it an unforgettable experience for every participant.

Akshat Choubey

Being the part of the conference, this year has been an overwhelming feeling for me, I can promise that this can be a life-changing experience for all the people out there because RYLMUN has stored a lot of things for them this year!

From delegating in the MUN to organizing it now, I have learned so many important lessons and grown so much as a person, I can't explain my gratitude in words. And the best part? It only gets better from here.
